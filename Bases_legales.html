Legal bases about TraderOS

The relationship between the user and TraderOS is based on the TraderOS Account Agreement that will be sign with your install and KYC/KYB process. Please note that your status (Natural Person, Legal Entity) will be based on the option you selected during your first order purchase, depending on whether you purchased it as a Natural Person or a Legal Entity. You can request a change only once, after successfully completing your TraderOS challenge and verification, or following your Reward payment as an TraderOS trader, but before starting your next trading cycle. The TraderOS Account Agreement is a legally binding document defining both our and your duties and rights. If you are interested in a sample of the contract, please contact us at Trader@os.com

Rules

Copy trading

You can copy trades from or to your personal account, another account, or even between multiple TraderOS accounts, (As long as these are your own trades from your own account).

There is only one restriction:

You cannot copy trades (or use the same trading ideas) between multiple TraderOS evaluation accounts. Each evaluation must be traded independently.

Crypto bumping

Bump and dump is a form of market manipulation where traders artificially inflate the price of a cryptocurrency by spreading false information, creating hype, or through coordinated buying.

While bump and dump can be profitable if executed correctly, there are significant risks involved. Market manipulation is illegal in many jurisdictions and can result in heavy fines or even criminal charges. Additionally, participating in bump and dump schemes can damage your reputation within the crypto community and lead to potential bans from trading platforms.
